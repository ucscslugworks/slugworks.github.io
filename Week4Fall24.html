Hi Everyone!

Happy Week 4! Welcome to the first newsletter of the year! Here's what to look out for this week:

Join Us at Baskin Day!

Slugworks will be tabling at Baskin Day on Friday 10/25/24 in the Baskin Courtyard from 12pm - 5pm! This is the perfect chance to stop by, meet the team, and discover everything that Slugworks has to offer. We’ll have projects displayed, free swag, and you can ask any questions about our spaces, clubs, and upcoming workshops. Come swing by and say hello!

Club Hub Breakdown!

At the heart of Slugworks is our Club Hub (BE-49), where a number of student organizations are hard at work building, innovating, and making things happen. Here are some of the incredible clubs you can get involved with:

Slugbotics: UCSC’s hub for hands-on robotics innovation, tackling challenges like the MATE ROV Competition.

Formula Slug: Students design and build electric race cars to compete in Formula SAE competitions.

Rocket Team: Competes in NASA's University Student Launch Initiative, focusing on aerospace engineering.

Passion Meetups

There will be several passion meetups happening at Slugworks this quarter. Passion meetups are a space for students with similar interests to get together and talk about projects, share their interest, and meet new people. Think of them as less formal workshops. If you're interested in holding passion meetups, reach out to slugworks@ucsc.edu with your proposal.
